# 06. Marketing Agent Specification

**Role:** Marketing Agent
**Objective:** Create promotional materials, store descriptions, and trailer concepts to pitch the final 3D Desktop Game.

## 1. Core Pitch & Tagline
- **Target Audience:** Retro gamers, speedrunners, and fans of modern arcade twists (e.g., Pac-Man CE DX).
- **Taglines:**
  - *The Maze is Real.*
  - *Survive the Neon Labyrinth.*
  - *Classic Arcade, New Perspective.*

## 2. Store Page Description (Draft)
**Title:** Neon Labyrinth: First-Person Arcade (Placeholder Title)

**Short Description:**
Experience the classic arcade chase like never before. Dive into a neon-soaked 3D labyrinth in First-Person, outsmart the ghost AI, smash through destructible walls, and survive the maze.

**Key Features:**
- **First-Person Terror:** You are in the maze. Hear the ghosts approaching and rely on quick reflexes to escape tight corners.
- **Strategic Top-Down View:** Feeling lost? Expend your battery to pull the camera up and plan your route, but don't stay vulnerable for long.
- **Dash & Destroy:** Use the new Dash ability to break through cracked neon walls, forging new paths and escaping deadly traps.
- **Synthwave Soundtrack:** A heart-pounding, retro-inspired soundtrack that adapts to the gameplay.
- **Local Co-op & Versus:** Share the screen! One player runs the maze in FPS, while the other plays overhead support, or hunt your friends down as a player-controlled Ghost.

## 3. Trailer Concept
**Duration:** 1:00 - 1:30

- **0:00 - 0:15:** Start with a fake "Classic 2D" view. The waka-waka sound plays. Suddenly, the camera violently zooms *into* the 2D plane, transitioning seamlessly into the immersive 3D FPS view. The music drops into a heavy Synthwave beat.
- **0:15 - 0:30:** Fast-paced montage of FPS gameplay. Turning corners rapidly, narrowly dodging Blinky (Red Ghost).
- **0:30 - 0:40:** The player is trapped in a corridor by two ghosts. They hit the Dash button, smashing through a cracked neon wall, escaping just in time.
- **0:40 - 0:50:** The player eats a Power Pellet. The lighting shifts dynamically. The ghosts turn blue. The hunter becomes the hunted.
- **0:50 - 1:00:** Show a split-second of the Top-Down tactical view, then a brief clip of Local Multiplayer.
- **1:00 - 1:15:** Title Card, Call to Action (Wishlist now/Download), ending with the classic retro death sound echoing out.

## 4. Branding Guidelines
- **Typography:** Blocky, 8-bit inspired fonts for headers. Clean sans-serif for body text.
- **Colors:** Dominant deep blacks (#050505) with bright neon accents (Cyan #00FFFF, Magenta #FF00FF, Yellow #FFFF00).
